An Interactive Social Community For All Muslim Women

We are a social community of female muslims. We come together to educate each other on issues related to our religion and take part in vital discussions to help strenghten our deen. We also donate monthly to orphanages and contribute to the development of mosques and our community in general.

Become A Part Of Us!

If you are a female muslim, you are automatically a member! Female muslims all over the world are welcomed to become part of this great movement, no matter where you come from, your skin color or what language you speak, everyone is welcomed here!

Our Story

Muslimah was created to enable the connection of female muslims all over the world, to make them know they are not alone and each and everyone of us are together! To answer their questions about the religion, to strengthen their faith and to contribute and give to the society, donate to orphanages, build mosques and madrasahs and in General, improve the muslim society and the world at large!

How We Meet

We have Unique Discord servers where we all discuss issues that are meant to be discord, connect, and talk to each other. We host video conference calls once every month, and broadcast live on our YouTube channel twice every month, inviting scholars to lecture us on chosen topics. We also meet physically, at least twice a year at an agreed location and the event will also be broadcasted live for those that can’t attend.
